kidsweather.com - Audience

Audience

The content of this weather forecasting website is the focus on children aged under 13 years and people with auditive deficiency. Groups: - kids in general - Educational institutions - hearing impaired The target scenarios: For use in educational institutions, the website can be used so that elementary school teachers can teach children in a simple, intuitive and fun way. The education department will be able to inform the school calendar based on weather forecasts. Delivery of warning messages of tornadoes, snowstorms or heavy rains. Provide suggestions for clothes for children for each type of weather. Use of sound for each type of weather to create accessibility for children audition disabilities.
